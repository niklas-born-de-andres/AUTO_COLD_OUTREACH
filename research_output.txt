## Meet the new companies joining this EIT Health programme to help healthcare innovators grow beyond their home markets

The supported start-ups are working on a range of innovations, like two different solutions for screening and protecting blood samples, a filter that cleans a patient‚Äôs blood so that it can be reinfused back into their body, systems for remote monitoring of everything from epileptic seizures to pacemakers, and improvements in medical imaging through AI ‚Äìamong others.
